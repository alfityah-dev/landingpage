Official Legal Documents

Foundation Information

Foundation Name: Yayasan Insan Fityah Indonesia

Legal Status: Registered Foundation

Registration Number: AHU-0026317.AH.01.04 Year 2021

Issued By: Minister of Law and Human Rights of the Republic of Indonesia

Establishment Year: 2021

Foundation Objectives

Yayasan Insan Fityah Indonesia was established with the following main objectives:

  • To organize and develop Islamic education, particularly in Quran memorization and Sharia knowledge
  • To facilitate community access to Islamic religious learning
  • To support da'wah activities in spreading Islamic teachings
  • To empower the community through educational and social programs
  • To establish and manage FIQRA (Fiqih and Quranic Academy) as the educational division

Foundation Activities

The foundation conducts various activities including:

  • Quran memorization programs and scholarships
  • Islamic studies and Fiqh education
  • Digital da'wah and Islamic content development
  • Social welfare programs and community assistance
  • Ramadan special programs and charitable activities
  • Collaboration with Islamic scholars and teachers

Legal Compliance

Yayasan Insan Fityah Indonesia operates in full compliance with Indonesian laws and regulations governing foundations and charitable organizations. The foundation is committed to transparency, accountability, and good governance in all its operations and programs.

All donations and zakat collections are managed according to Islamic principles and Indonesian legal requirements, ensuring proper distribution to eligible beneficiaries and program implementation.
